Introducing 'Daddy Pocket' (Daddy's Easy Pocket Money) – the easy, intuitive pocket money tracking and card payment reminder app designed specifically for dads!

Have you ever given up on budgeting because of complex bank syncing, infinite category options, or confusing charts?
'Daddy Pocket' eliminates unnecessary clutter and focuses entirely on the essential features that dads actually need.

■ Key Features
1. **My Remaining Budget at a Glance**
- Set your monthly pocket money budget, and see your remaining balance displayed clearly on the home screen.
- No complex math needed – just open the app and see how much you can spend.

2. **Simple Expense Logs**
- Quickly log expenses by selecting the amount, category, and payment method (cash/card).
- Minimizing input fields makes daily tracking effortless.

3. **Fixed Expense Automation**
- Register recurring monthly bills such as mobile plans, streaming subscriptions, and car insurance. The app will automatically log them on the first day of each month.

4. **Card Payment Reminders**
- Displays a warning banner at the top of the Home screen 3 days prior to your credit card payment dates and sends push notifications.
- Never miss a payment deadline again.

5. **Clean Analytics & Settings**
- Review your spending habits categorized in a clean pie chart.
- Update your monthly budget anytime with a simple slider.

■ Secure & Private (Local-First)
- All your spending data and credit card details are stored securely inside your device's local database (Room/SQLite).
- We do not transmit or collect any of your personal data on external servers – 100% offline and secure.

Download 'Daddy Pocket' today and start stress-free, simple budget management!
